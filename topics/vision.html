Vision

The art of seeing the invisible and believing in the impossible

Vision without action is a daydream. Action without vision is a nightmare.

To accomplish great things we must dream as well as act.

If I have seen farther than others, it is because I was standing on the shoulders of giants.

The most pathetic person in the world is someone who has sight but no vision.

Vision is the art of seeing what is invisible to others.

Your vision will become clear only when you can look into your own heart. Who looks outside, dreams; who looks inside, awakes.

A vision is not just a picture of what could be; it is an appeal to our better selves, a call to become something more.

Good business leaders create a vision, articulate the vision, passionately own the vision, and relentlessly drive it to completion.

The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams.

Vision is the capacity to see beyond the immediate, to imagine what might be, and to inspire others to help make it so.

Where there is no vision, the people perish.

A clear vision, backed by definite plans, gives you a tremendous feeling of confidence and personal power.

Vision is not enough; it must be combined with venture. It is not enough to stare up the steps; we must step up the stairs.

The greatest achievement was at first and for a time a dream. The oak sleeps in the acorn.

Vision looks inward and becomes duty. Vision looks outward and becomes aspiration. Vision looks upward and becomes faith.

You need a vision, and you need to be able to articulate that vision in a way that's compelling and exciting to other people.

A leader has the vision and conviction that a dream can be achieved. He inspires the power and energy to get it done.

Vision is the ability to talk about the future with such clarity it is as if we are talking about the past.

The very essence of leadership is that you have to have a vision. It's got to be a vision you articulate clearly and forcefully on every occasion.

Vision is not simply seeing what is. It is seeing what could be, and then having the courage to make it so.

Your vision will become your life's trajectory. Aim carefully.

A man without a vision for his future always returns to his past.

Vision without execution is hallucination. Execution without vision is drudgery.

The power of vision is that it gives you a reason to get out of bed when you don't feel like it.

Great vision without great people is irrelevant.

Vision is the compass that guides through the storms of doubt and the deserts of discouragement.

Your personal vision is your lighthouse in the fog of daily distractions.

A compelling vision is the starting point for all great achievements, but it's the daily commitment that makes it real.

Vision is the bridge between the world as it is and the world as it could be.

The clearest vision often comes from the deepest reflection.
